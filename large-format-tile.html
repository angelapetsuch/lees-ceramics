What is Large Format Tile?

The latest tile technology has introduced a new breed of ceramic tile installation: Large Format Tile. These impressive 4’ x 10’ ceramic tile panels provide a beautiful, seamless look that architects and clients love. Large format tile is perfect for creating a modern, monolithic appearance in both residential and commercial spaces.

Why Choose Lee’s Ceramics?

  • Our experienced team is fully trained in handling, transporting, and installing large format panels.
  • We use specialized equipment and techniques to ensure flawless results.
  • We deliver the skill, patience, and attention to detail these installations demand.

Large format tile installation is not a job for amateurs. It requires careful planning, a skilled hand, and the right tools. Lee’s Ceramics brings all of this and more to your project, ensuring your space looks stunning and stands the test of time.
